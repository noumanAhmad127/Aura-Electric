About Aura Electric

For over 40 years AURA ELECTRIC has been and continues to be, a dynamic leader in the electrical supply and lighting fixture industry.

WELCOME TO AURA ELECTRIC SUPPLY, your comprehensive resource for a complete range of electrical and lighting supplies.

Founded in 1980 as a small retail business, Aura has evolved as a recognized leader in the electrical supply and lighting industry, with a tremendous inventory and unparalleled service. From large-scale contractors to local electricians and homeowners, hundreds of loyal customers know and trust the friendly and knowledgeable staff at Aura Electric Supply to make sure they get the right item at the right price.
